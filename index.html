About Me

The work. Nine years on Eli Lilly's enterprise design system. Researcher, designer, Scrum Master, Product Owner, lead architect — I've held nearly every role because I've always believed you make better design decisions when you understand everything surrounding them.

The approach. Deep discovery before solutions. Accessibility as a baseline, not an afterthought. Staying in the room through the build — not tossing work over the wall.

Outside of work. Boulderer, street photographer, reader. Happiest somewhere unfamiliar.
