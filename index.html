About

My path into UX started with obsessively exploring settings menus in video games. I was fascinated by how small interface choices could completely change an experience.

That instinct carried through an agency career, into enterprise product design at Eli Lilly — where I've led design systems evolution, acted as Product Owner, served as Scrum Master, and rebuilt documentation infrastructure for 100k+ users across 50+ pharmaceutical brands.

What drives me is the design problem underneath the design problem. I bring discernment, curiosity, and the patience to actually understand a system before proposing to change it.

Outside of work: street photography, Stardew Valley, audiobooks, and a deep affection for well-placed negative space.
