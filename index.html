About Me

My manager had a name for a certain kind of project — the ones where something had gone sideways, the scope was murky, or the problem didn't fit neatly into existing processes. She threw those at me. Not because I was the most senior, but because I slow down before jumping to solutions and tend to be a stabilizing presence when things get complicated.

My approach centers on discernment, curiosity, and groundedness. I try to understand what everyone is actually bringing to a problem before deciding what needs to happen. I steer toward process over blame — if there's a gap, I'd rather build something to fill it.

9+ years on Eli Lilly's design system team. Researcher, designer, Scrum Master, Product Owner — I've held nearly every role because I believe you make better design decisions when you understand what surrounds them.
